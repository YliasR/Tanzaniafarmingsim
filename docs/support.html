Common Questions

My sensor node isn't sending SMS

Check that the SIM card has credit and the SIM800L module's power LED is blinking. Make sure the antenna is connected. If the LED blinks rapidly, the module can't register with the network — try moving the node to a spot with better signal.

The AI advisory seems inaccurate

Verify your sensor calibration, especially pH and moisture. The AI relies on the data it receives — if readings are off, the advice will be too. You can recalibrate by following the setup guide included with your node.

How do I add more nodes to my account?

Submit a quote request on our contact & pricing page, or open a support ticket and mention your existing node IDs. We'll configure the new nodes to work with your existing setup.

Can I use my own AI model?

Yes! Set USE_LOCAL_AI=true in your server .env file and point it at your local Ollama instance. Any model that supports the chat completions format will work.
